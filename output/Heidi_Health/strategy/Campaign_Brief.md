# Campaign Brief

## Company
Heidi Health

## Audience
Clinicians and care teams who want to spend less time on documentation and more time with patients.

### Founder brand learning (human priority)
- Tone should be clinical but deeply empathetic
- Avoid using the word AI in consumer-facing copy

## 7-Day Goals
- Day 1-2: Establish narrative tension and urgency.
- Day 3-4: Build trust with proof and founder POV.
- Day 5-6: Convert attention into response and meetings.
- Day 7: Reinforce mission and long-term positioning.
