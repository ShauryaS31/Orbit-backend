# Campaign Brief: Relevance AI

## Campaign Objective

- **Business goal:** Increase demo bookings from AI operations teams by 20%
- **Success metric:** Click-through rate on LinkedIn carousel and demo CTA

## Strategic Thesis

Relevance AI should win this cycle by showing that category expertise can translate into immediate operational leverage. The campaign thesis is to combine technical authority with a concrete conversion path: prove buyer-relevant outcomes in high-credibility channels, then reduce friction to the Build your AI workforce action with explicit context for implementation and decision confidence.

## Target Audience

- **Primary buyer:** Operations leader scaling processes
- **Secondary buyer:** Growth manager automating outreach and qualification
- **Buying context:** Evaluating options while balancing delivery commitments, internal alignment, and risk controls.
- **Trigger moments:** Teams stuck with one-off automations that do not scale
