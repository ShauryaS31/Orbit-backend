# Relevance AI LinkedIn Style Playbook

## 1. Brand Summary

Relevance AI’s LinkedIn presence targets **GTM operators, builders, and agent-curious teams** in a **purple / violet / magenta / dark-navy** visual world with **pixel-art, arcade, and “Level Up”** energy—event posters, bootcamp tiles, and community-forward storytelling. This is **not** clinical healthcare editorial and **not** butter-yellow Heidi cards.

## 2. Visual Identity

- **High-energy event-card** and **product-launch** aesthetics.
- **Pixel art**, arcade motifs, **Relevance Live**, **Agents@Work**, **AI Ops Bootcamp** vibes.
- **Speaker-forward** layouts and **lineup** grids where appropriate.
- **Operator community** feel—peers building agents, not enterprise stock photography.

## 3. Color Palette

- **Core:** purple, violet, magenta, electric blue accents.
- **Depth:** dark navy / near-black backgrounds for contrast.
- **Highlights:** neon-adjacent accents **within brand** (not random rainbow gradients).
- **Avoid:** Heidi pale yellow fields, clinical cream editorial, generic pastel SaaS gradients.

## 4. Typography Direction

- **Bold, franchise-like headlines**—short, punchy, game/event poster rhythm.
- **Mono or pixel-flavored** display type acceptable when readable.
- Supporting text stays **short**—never long paragraphs in the image.

## 5. Common Post Formats

- **Event / conference card** — Relevance Live, meetup, agenda teaser.
- **Bootcamp / workshop** — AI Ops Bootcamp, build sessions, cohort energy.
- **Community / Level Up** — operator peer proof, stylized avatars (not photoreal stock).
- **Product / platform** — agent builder, GTM stack—stylized UI fragments without fake enterprise logos.

## 6. Image Composition Rules

- **1080×1080** square-native framing.
- **Visible text: short**—headline + optional sub-hook; **3–10 words** typical.
- **No long paragraphs** in the raster.
- **Stylized human pixel avatars** OK; **no photoreal humanoid robots** or power armor unless explicitly briefed as stylized pixel character.

## 7. Copy Voice

- Builder-native, direct, playful-but-smart.
- **GTM operators**, **agents**, **shipping**, **live build** language.
- Avoid sleepy corporate tone; avoid clinical healthcare wording.

## 8. Proof / Claim Rules

- **Do not invent statistics** for on-image display.
- Prefer **qualitative franchise lines** and **event truth** over fake metrics.
- Captions can reference outcomes—must not fabricate numbers.

## 9. What To Avoid

- **Heidi-style** butter yellow / cream **clinical editorial** cards as the primary look.
- Generic blue-gradient SaaS hero blobs.
- Photoreal **humanoid robots**, mech suits, chrome androids—unless stylized pixel art per brand.
- Dense legal or paragraph text inside the image.

## 10. Good Visual Directions

- “Dark navy field + magenta pixel border + ‘Relevance Live’ headline.”
- “Arcade marquee + speaker lineup silhouettes (abstract, not real faces).”
- “Level Up poster with mono headline and pixel star accents.”

## 11. Bad Visual Directions

- “Pale yellow report card with burgundy serif” (wrong brand).
- “Hospital editorial proof tile.”
- “75% ROI” giant on-image stat without source.

## 12. Image Prompt Contract

1. This playbook is **source of truth** for Relevance LinkedIn **image** generation.
2. **Square 1:1** composition for LinkedIn.
3. Use **only provided visible text** strings for readable words in-image.
4. **No fake logos**; **no fake partner marks**.
5. **No invented stats** in visible type.
6. Lean **purple / pixel / arcade / event** energy—not clinical yellow/cream.

## 13. Caption Contract

- Punchy, operator-to-operator; can reference events and community.
- Avoid healthcare-clinical phrasing unless the post is explicitly about a healthcare customer story.

## 14. Example Post Concepts

- **Level Up** — pixel frame, dark navy, violet headline.
- **Relevance Live** — event card, agenda hints, speaker blocks abstracted.
- **Agents@Work** — enterprise tile energy without fake logos.
- **AI Ops Bootcamp** — bootcamp poster, cohort/build language.

### Preferred short on-image phrases

- “Level Up”
- “Relevance Live”
- “Agents@Work”
- “AI Ops Bootcamp”
- “GTM Operators Building Agents”
